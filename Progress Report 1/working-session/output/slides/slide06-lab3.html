librosa STFT

Team: Cameron's POC, cervanj2 architecture

Audio → Spectrograms

  • STFT = Short-Time Fourier Transform
  • 1D waveform → 2D spectrogram (frequency × time)
  • Reversible with ISTFT (perfect reconstruction)
  • U-Net processes spectrograms like images

The Transformation:

Waveform: (661,500,) → Spectrogram: (1,025 × 646)

1,025 frequency bins × 646 time frames = 662,150 measurements

Why it matters: Core of the POC. Enables neural networks to "see" audio. STFT in preprocessing, ISTFT in post-processing.
